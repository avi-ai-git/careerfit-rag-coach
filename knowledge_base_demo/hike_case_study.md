# Case Study: HIKE Hochschulinkubator Nordhausen — AI Education + Marketing
*Referred to as "Regional Innovation Hub" in anonymized materials.*

## Organization

A state-funded startup incubator and entrepreneurship support center in Thuringia, eastern Germany. Part of the StarTH network of startup support institutions. Serves early-stage founders, students, and the regional startup ecosystem.

## Demo Candidate's Role

AI Education Lead and Marketing Content Manager (combined role)
Duration: November 2024 to December 2025 (14 months)

---

## Context and Challenge

The organization needed to introduce generative AI tools to a non-technical audience of founders and students — people with no prior programming background, working in German-speaking eastern Germany where English-language AI education was essentially absent.

At the same time, the organization faced an acute institutional threat: a funding situation that put its continued operation at risk. It needed to make the case for its value to regional and state-level decision-makers quickly, using whatever communication tools were available.

There was no existing AI education infrastructure, no content production workflow, and no systematic way for staff or residents to keep up with the pace of AI tool development.

---

## What Demo Candidate Did

### AI Education Program — Six Formats, 120+ Participants

#### 1. NotebookLM Session — HIKE Lab
Live session introducing Google NotebookLM to founders and students. Hands-on workflow: upload documents, generate audio overviews, build research notebooks. Purpose: give non-technical participants a zero-friction entry point to AI-assisted research. Recorded and published on YouTube.

#### 2. Vibe Coding with GenAI — HIKE Lab
Live session on building small functional tools without traditional coding experience, using Claude and similar tools as the development layer. Demonstrated that founders without engineering backgrounds could ship working prototypes. Recorded and published on YouTube.

#### 3. AI Music Production — HIKE Lab
Live session on using AI tools for audio and music production. Covered generative audio, stem manipulation, and AI-assisted composition. Extended the AI education programme into creative domains, relevant to media and cultural startup founders. Recorded and published on YouTube.

#### 4. 2-Day English GenAI Bootcamp
**First English-language GenAI bootcamp in the state of Thuringia.**

- 14 participants. 2-day intensive format. Certificates issued.
- Curriculum: prompt engineering fundamentals, tool selection and evaluation, AI workflow design for non-technical users, hands-on practice with Claude, Midjourney, ChatGPT, Runway.
- Deliverable for participants: the AI Bootcamp Prompt Engineering Knowledge Hub (Notion), a structured, self-service reference covering 50+ tools by category. Participants continue using this after the bootcamp ends.
- Design principle: every session required participants to apply skills to their own actual business problem, not a synthetic exercise.

#### 5. Female Founders Retreat 2025 — Rittergut Positz
GenAI coaching delivered to 30+ female founders at an off-site retreat. Single-day format. Audience: early-stage female entrepreneurs from across the region. Focus: practical tool adoption, demystifying AI, identifying highest-leverage use cases for each participant's business context.

#### 6. HIKEathon 2024 + 2025
Annual AI + design thinking event. Coaching role in both 2024 and 2025 editions.

- 80 total participants across both editions. Direct coaching group of 9.
- Format: structured sprints combining AI tools with design thinking methodology.
- Participant outputs: validated AI-assisted prototypes or concept refinements within the event timeframe.

---

### Marketing and Advocacy

#### #hikemussbleiben Campaign
("hike must remain" — responding directly to the funding threat)

- Produced a multi-channel advocacy campaign including video content, social copy, and stakeholder-facing materials.
- Videos were used directly in grant applications submitted to regional and state-level authorities.
- **Outcome: campaign contributed to securing €1.2M in public funding for the StarTH network** (of which HIKE is a part) from the Thuringian government ministry. HIKE is still operating.
- This is the highest-stakes content production outcome in Demo Candidate's career to date.

#### Social Media and Content Strategy
- Grew organization's LinkedIn from baseline to 1,000+ followers.
- Grew Instagram from baseline to 800+ followers.
- Maintained consistent editorial calendar across 14 months, bilingual where required.

#### HIKE Demo Day 2025 Campaign
Full event campaign: pre-event content, live coverage, post-event materials. Coordinated across internal stakeholders and partner organizations.

#### AI Tool of the Month Series
Ongoing educational series for the founder community: one AI tool per month, practical write-up, hands-on examples. Built the organization's reputation as a credible AI education source in the region.

### AI Film Production

- Produced AI short film "CTRL+ALT+DEITY.EXE" during tenure, as a demonstration of generative AI creative production at a professional standard.
- Logline: "A grieving priest feeds the memory of his dead daughter into an AI god that promises to erase pain, until a forbidden cassette forces the system to remember."
- Production stack: ChatGPT + Claude (story development), Midjourney + NanoBanana + DALL-E 3 (image generation), Veo + Kling + Runway ML + Higgsfield (motion and video synthesis), ElevenLabs (voice and audio), Adobe Premiere Pro (final assembly).
- Selected for the **Artefact AI Film Festival Paris 2025**.
