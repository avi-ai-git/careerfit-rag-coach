# Application Examples — Tone, Positioning, Cover Letters, and Interview Answers

## How to Use This File

This file gives CareerFit RAG Coach stable examples of the candidate's preferred application style.

The assistant should learn these principles:
- Be specific, not generic.
- Use evidence from the knowledge base.
- Avoid inflated claims.
- Acknowledge gaps when relevant.
- Position the candidate at the intersection of AI, content, education, creative production, and communication.
- Keep the tone professional, human, and direct.
- Do not oversell pure engineering ability.

This file is especially useful for:
- `generate_application_positioning`
- `generate_interview_prep`
- cover-letter drafting
- outreach message drafting
- interview talking points

### Result

14 participants completed the bootcamp, and the prompt library enabled 25+ non-technical users to self-serve AI workflows. Across all six education formats, 120+ participants were reached over 14 months. The project also helped position the organization as an AI education actor in the regional startup ecosystem.

# Style Guide for Generated Applications

## Preferred Tone

- Clear
- Grounded
- Specific
- Human
- Confident but not inflated
- Strategic, not desperate
- Honest about gaps

## Avoid

- Generic enthusiasm without evidence
- Buzzword stacking
- Overclaiming engineering experience
- Saying "passionate" too often
- Pretending every role is a perfect fit
- Long paragraphs with no proof points
- Claims that are not supported by the KB

## Strong Phrases

- "turning AI experimentation into repeatable workflows"
- "helping non-technical users adopt AI tools practically"
- "bridging communication, education, and applied AI"
- "source-grounded and honest about gaps"
- "from one-off prompting to reusable systems"
- "AI adoption only works when workflows are understandable and repeatable"

## Weak / Overused Phrases

- "I am passionate about AI"
- "I am a perfect fit"
- "I have extensive technical expertise"
- "I revolutionized"
- "cutting-edge visionary"
- "AI guru"
- "10x productivity"
- "expert in everything"
