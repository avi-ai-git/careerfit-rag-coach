# Case Study: International Development Agency — LMS and Digital Transformation
*Anonymized as "Client A" / "International Development Agency" for demo.*

## Organization

A major German international development agency operating globally. The specific project was based in Bonn, Germany, and focused on internal digital transformation and staff training infrastructure.

## Demo Candidate's Role

Digital Transformation and LMS Intern
Location: Bonn, Germany

---

## Context and Challenge

The organization's internal training programs were underperforming. Staff engagement with existing learning resources was low, and the LMS (Learning Management System) was not being used effectively. The organization also needed to secure additional internal funding to continue expanding the digital transformation program.

---

## What Demo Candidate Did

### LMS Redesign and Implementation

- Contributed to the redesign and implementation of the organization's Learning Management System.
- Improved UX, content structure, and onboarding flow for new users.
- Result: **30% lift in user engagement** measured after implementation.

### Funding Pitch

- Produced a video pitch presenting the digital transformation program's value and future roadmap to internal decision-makers.
- Result: **50% additional funding secured** from internal stakeholders as a direct outcome of the pitch.

### Digital Transformation Support

- Contributed to planning documentation, knowledge management processes, and internal communications related to the digital transformation initiative.
